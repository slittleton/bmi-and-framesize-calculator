Frame Size Information

Your frame size affects what a healthy bmi is for your body type. If you have a small frame it could mean that you are not underweight if you are below 18.5. If you have a large body frame it possible that you are not overweight even if your bmi is a bit above 25. Other factors to consider are your level of muscularity. If you have a lot of muscle then bmi is not as good of a measure for a healthy weight.
